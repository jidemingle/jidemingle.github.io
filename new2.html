Insights

Sales Performance: The dashboard reveals that specific companies such as Chevrolet and Ford contribute significantly to the overall Revenue indicating a strong market position for these two companies. Though, these two carcompanies i.e Chevrolet and Ford do lead in the Year-to-date sales, they however do not have a strong YOY sales growth .Furthermore, it can be seen that Chevrolet and ford, the leading companies in YTD Sales revenue have a significantly low YTD Average prices. The dashboard also reveals which regions are driving the most sales and contributing significantly to overall revenue. With this we can understand regional differences in customer preferences and tailor products or services accordingly.

Consumer Preferences: We are also able to Identify the most popular body styles and color among consumers. This is important in helping us track changes in consumer preferences over time, such as a growing preference for SUVs. Ultimately, this will help Inform decisions about new product development.

Trends and Opportunities: The YTD Sales weekly trend provides insights into identifying unexpected sales fluctuations, emerging trends or changes in consumer preferences. We can Identify recurring peaks and troughs in sales related to specific times of the year. Overall, this information helps to optimize production, inventory, and marketing efforts.

Conclusion: This Power BI project not only sheds light on the sales performance of the various automobile companies, but also highlights the crucial areas (regions, body style, color) where efforts must be concentrated. By identifying the economic indicators, market trends, consumer preferences, and competitive landscape as well as pricing and promotion, we can make informed decisions about product development, marketing strategies, pricing, and inventory management. Additionally, this analysis can help identify emerging trends and opportunities in the automotive market.
